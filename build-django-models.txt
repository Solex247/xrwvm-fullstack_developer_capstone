Build CarModel and CarMake
Django Models
Estimated time: 90 minutes
A dealership typically manages cars from one or more makers or manufacturers,
and customers should be allowed to review the cars they purchased from a dealer.
In this lab, you will create the CarModel  and CarMake  models in the Django app.
A car model  includes basic information, such as its make, year, type, and
dealer ID.
A car make  includes basic information, such as name and description.
